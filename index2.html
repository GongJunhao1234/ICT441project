Our History

  • 2012  -

  • The school accelerated the Digital transformation of education, assisted in the cultivation of first-class talents and scientific research, connected the "ring" interconnection cable between campuses, enabled the subject intelligent computing service sharing platform (Phase II), and promoted the IPv6 based campus Internet of Things, four center integration, 5G campus construction and AI+education AI application scenario construction.

  • At present, the school has signed intercollegiate cooperation agreements with 251 universities or institutions in 55 countries and regions, with 2837 international students studying in 153 countries and regions worldwide, including 2229 international degree students.
